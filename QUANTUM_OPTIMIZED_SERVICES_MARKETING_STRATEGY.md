# Quantum Optimized Services - Marketing Launch Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive go-to-market strategy for the launch of Lumen Helix's new **Quantum Optimized Services** offering. This premium service tier positions the company as a leader in quantum-inspired computing and QUBO-based optimization, targeting enterprise clients in healthcare, finance, and operations management.

**Launch Date:** February 2026  
**Target Audience:** Enterprise tech leaders, healthcare organizations, financial institutions, operational planning teams  
**Primary Value Proposition:** 30x performance improvements over classical optimization through quantum-inspired QUBO formulations

- Weekly metrics review (Weeks 1-4 of launch)
