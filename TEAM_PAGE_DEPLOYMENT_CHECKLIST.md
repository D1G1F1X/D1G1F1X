### Week 3: Launch Success

- **WARN**: 3,000-5,000 visits, 80-120 leads, 10-20 bookings

## Contingency Actions

### If Traffic is Low
1. Increase LinkedIn ad spend by 50%
2. Contact existing clients for testimonials/case studies
3. Have sales team reach out to prospects mentioning team page
4. Create urgency with "limited time team consultation offer"
5. Adjust messaging based on click analysis

### If Lead Quality is Poor
1. Implement stricter lead scoring criteria
2. Conduct qualification calls with first 10 leads
3. Adjust targeting parameters in paid ads
4. Refine CTA copy and landing page messaging
5. Create industry-specific nurture sequences

### If Engagement is Low
1. Review team member content for resonance
2. Test different posting times and frequency
3. Increase engagement with comments/replies
4. Create more interactive content (polls, questions)
5. Leverage team members for personal amplification

### If Sales Issues Arise
1. Conduct sales team feedback session
2. Refine discovery questions
3. Create objection handling guides
4. Provide additional training on team expertise
5. Increase sales leadership involvement
