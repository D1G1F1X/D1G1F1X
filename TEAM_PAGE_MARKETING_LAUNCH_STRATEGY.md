# TEAM PAGE MARKETING LAUNCH STRATEGY
## Lumen Helix Solutions - Team & Leadership Showcase

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The new Team page represents a strategic opportunity to humanize Lumen Helix Solutions by showcasing the co-founder expertise, in-house capabilities, and integrated support structure. This launch strategy focuses on positioning the company as a team of experts rather than a solo consultancy, emphasizing the depth of talent supporting client projects.

**Launch Timeline:** Immediate (2-4 week rollout)
**Target Audience:** Enterprise clients, healthcare providers, AI researchers, venture investors
**Primary Goal:** Increase trust, credibility, and lead quality through transparent team visibility

---

## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES

1. **Build Trust & Credibility** - Showcase proven expertise through detailed co-founder profiles
2. **Differentiate Service Offerings** - Highlight in-house + external specialist model as competitive advantage
3. **Support Sales Conversations** - Provide tangible evidence of 24/7 support and enterprise capabilities
4. **Drive Lead Quality** - Attract ideal-fit clients who value team expertise and transparency
5. **Enhance SEO Authority** - Add keyword-rich content (team members, specialties, services integration)

## SUCCESS METRICS & KPIs

### Primary Metrics
- **Traffic:** Team page views and session duration
- **Engagement:** CTAs clicked (services, consultations, contact)
- **Conversion:** Consultation bookings attributed to Team page
- **Authority:** Backlinks to Team page from industry sites

### Secondary Metrics
- **Lead Quality:** Score increase for Team page visitors vs. others
- **Content Engagement:** Blog clicks-through from Team page
- **Social Impact:** Mentions of founders by external sources
- **SEO:** Keyword rankings for team member names + expertise

### Target Benchmarks (First 60 days)
- 500+ Team page visits
- 15-20% CTA click-through rate
- 5-10 consultation bookings
- 2-3 industry article features/mentions

## RISK MITIGATION

**Risk:** Team members receive unwanted outreach
**Mitigation:** Privacy settings on personal profiles, company email preferred

**Risk:** Founder availability for webinars/interviews
**Mitigation:** Pre-schedule in advance, record when needed, provide backup speakers

**Risk:** Competitor targeting Team page achievements
**Mitigation:** Continuous innovation and research output, thought leadership maintenance

**Risk:** Outdated team information
**Mitigation:** Quarterly page audit, automated reminders for updates
