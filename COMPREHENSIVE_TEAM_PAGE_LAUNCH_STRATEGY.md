The new Lumen Helix Team page transforms how we present organizational expertise to the market. By showcasing our executive leadership (Eric J. Buck as CTO/Sales Lead and Christopher Phillips as CIO/Administrative Lead with Navy veteran background), core technical talent (developers, cryptography specialist, game theory expert, UI/UX designer), and support infrastructure, we establish credibility with enterprise clients and differentiate ourselves as a full-service, well-resourced organization.

4. Communicate 24/7 support capability and operational excellence
5. Attract top technical talent through transparent team showcase

## Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

### Risk 1: Low Team Page Traffic
**Mitigation:** Increase paid advertising budget by 50%, enhance internal promotion, add team page link to homepage hero

### Risk 2: Lead Quality Issues
**Mitigation:** Implement strict lead scoring in CRM, conduct sales calls to validate, adjust targeting parameters

### Risk 3: Content Performance Underperformance
**Mitigation:** A/B test headlines and messaging, repurpose underperforming content, analyze competition

### Risk 4: Team Member Availability for Promotion
**Mitigation:** Pre-record content, schedule interviews in advance, create backup promotional materials
